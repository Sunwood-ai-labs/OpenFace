# Code of conduct

OpenFace follows the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

Be respectful, constructive, and specific. Harassment, discriminatory conduct, personal attacks, and publication of another person's private information are not acceptable. Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior harms the community.

Report conduct concerns privately through the Sunwood AI Labs organization profile. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with appropriate confidentiality.
