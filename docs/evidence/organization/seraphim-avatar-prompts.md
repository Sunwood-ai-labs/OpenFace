These four fictional identities were generated with the built-in image generation tool. Each final image uses a plain studio background and a square, circular-avatar-safe composition.

## Aurelia Vale

```text
Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: square organization member avatar for a professional AI safety platform
Primary request: Create a photorealistic head-and-shoulders portrait of Aurelia Vale, a fictional Japanese-British woman in her early 30s and lead AI safety researcher for an angel-themed organization.
Scene/backdrop: perfectly plain warm ivory studio background, no texture, no scenery.
Style/medium: premium editorial corporate photography, realistic skin texture, natural facial details, understated luxury.
Composition/framing: centered face, shoulders visible, direct but warm gaze, square crop with generous margin, suitable for a circular avatar.
Lighting/mood: soft diffused studio light, calm, intelligent, trustworthy.
Wardrobe/details: minimal cream blazer over a charcoal top; one very small matte-gold halo-ring lapel pin as a subtle angel motif.
Constraints: one person only, distinctive real human identity, no text, no logo, no watermark, no wings, no glowing halo, no fantasy costume, no gradients or patterned background, no frame or border.
```
